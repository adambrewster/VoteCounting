Step 2. Nominations

Enter the names of the candidates nominated. Optionally, you may add a short nickname for a candidate to make it easier to tally votes.

NicknameFull NameAbbreviations

Step 3. Tally Votes

Enter the names indicated on each ballot.

Names need not be entered exactly as listed on the ballot, but as a teller you must interpret the intent of the voter and ensure that the computer accurately records his or her intention.

You may abbreviate names as long as you specify enough to make the abbreviation unique. The shortest unique prefix of each name is calculated and shown to the right of the names of nominees.

Names matching the ballot you have entered are shown to the right of the input area.

Step 4. Report Results

When all of the ballots are entered, Submit the date to OpaVote to be counted.
